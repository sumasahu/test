Upsized Data Plus
Upsized Data Plus is only available to lines with an existing eligible contract on selected mobile or mobile broadband plans. Upon expiry of the applicable contract, additional Upsized Data Plus subscription fee of $8/month will be applicable, on top of existing Upsized Data Plus subscription fee of $11.80/month. Upon re-contract of mobile line or mobile broadband, additional Upsized Data Plus subscription fee of $8/month will be automatically removed. Each mobile or mobile broadband line may only be subscribed to Upsized Data, Upsized Data Plus or Upsized Data Super. Upon subscription to Upsized Data Plus, existing Upsized Data / Upsized Data Super (if any) will be automatically removed. When switching between Upsized Data, Upsized Data Plus or Upsized Data Super, (i) monthly subscription will not be pro-rated and (ii) registration charge will be waived.
